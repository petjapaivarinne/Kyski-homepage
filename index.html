KY-SKI

Aalto University Business Students' Skiing Club.

Since 1975

Aalto University Business Students' skiing club KY-Ski is one of the oldest clubs in the school. The main goal of the club is to spread the good spirit of skiing, not forgetting the after-ski. We organise cool events and trips from Vihti to Levi and Bad Gastein, with a sense of fun and relaxation! Surfing is also close to KY-Ski's heart and in the summer we organise the most relaxed trip of the year to the waves of Portugal.

KY-Ski is run by a board, which elects around seven new members every year. When you accept a seat on the board, you commit to the club for the next three years. However, to quote a wise old Kyski-member: "once a kyski, always a kyski". KY-Ski is a safe place for everyone to be themselves!

In KY-Ski, the downward season is a permanent trend!
